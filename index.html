What We Do

Eloolo Permaculture

The Eloolo Permaculture Initiative is a group of young environmentalist and activists engaged in education, research, consultation, implementation of food systems and associated Permaculture projects. We strive to improve human ecosystems to become healthy and abundant, guided by our ethics: Care for the people, care for the earth and fair share!

Clean Travel

The Clean Travel program is Namibia's first carbon offsetting offer. By donating a tree/s you help offset your carbon use/emissions as well as help educate children and communities.

Farm Okukuna

Farm Okukuna is a visionary new project to enhance food and nutrition security in the informal settlements of Windhoek.The farm act as the centre for a number of programmes.
