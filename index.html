What We Do

Eloolo Permaculture

The Eloolo Permaculture Initiative is a group of young environmentalist and activists engaged in education, research, consultation, implementation of food systems and associated Permaculture projects. We strive to improve human ecosystems to become healthy and abundant, guided by our ethics: Care for the people, care for the earth and fair share!

DIFFERENCE WE ARE MAKING

We are a Namibian NGO engaging in education, research, consultation, implementation of food systems and associated permaculture and community projects. We are creating small local changes but ones which are directly and indirectly influencing action in the wider environment, organic agriculture, appropriate technology, communities and other movements for a sustainable Namibia. We strive to educate the Namibian public at large, the benefits of long-term permaculture for the sustainability of our environment. We aim to achieve this through polarizing research that evaluates the state of our environment, engaging with like-minded organizations to come up with practical solutions, and implementing projects organized and coordinated at community level. THAT is the difference we take pride in. THAT is the difference we are making!
